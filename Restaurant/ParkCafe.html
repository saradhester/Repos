Restaurant Description

Welcome to Park Cafe! We're located directly across from the park. Making us perfect for your mid-afternoon snack, lunch, or dinner. Our menu is full of classics that have been remastered! Enjoy our cozy indoor atmosphere or dine out on the patio for a gorgous park view. Not too hungry? No Problem! We also serve a wide variety of coffee and tea perfect for sipping over your favorite book! If you are intersted in catering or a private party please call ahead or use the contact us link above to submit requests. We would love to host your next event!
